ADDIS VIEW VILLA

ADDIS VIEW VILLA

Complete interior design project for this 1600 sqm villa with breath-taking views to the city of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. To create this sophisticated interior scheme, we used a soft & light color palette which enhances the power of light in the space. The interior features bespoke furniture and chandeliers in combination with large format travertines, marbles and metal finishings resulting in a unique contemporary luxury look.
