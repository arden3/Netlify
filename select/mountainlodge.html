The Mountain Lodge

The Mountain Lodge is a cottage-styled residence located in the Northern mountains of Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia. The interior, conceived to bring to the family a place to relax and disconnect, is contemporary and focuses on comfort.

ABOUT THE Interior

For the interior, the materials used are woods in different textures and finishes, combined with deep brown marbles in natura finishes to blend into the mountainous atmosphere that surrounds this cottage.
