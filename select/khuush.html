Khuush Valley Residence

Khuush Valley is to be a tranquil gem amidst the bustling city of Ulaanbaatar, featuring classic inspired luxury villas arranged along the beautiful Khuush Valley, only 20 minutes from the city center. Mondreem emphasized on the design of the landscape to enhance the feeling of living amongst nature and seclusion, creating outdoor spaces for hiking, gardens and sports.
