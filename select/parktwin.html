Park Twin Tower

The elliptical shape of the towers creates unique spaces for the residents, while maximizing the views towards the national park of Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia. The development will feature high-end apartments with indoor pool, fitness and spa centre, as well as indoor shopping streets in the first three floors to allowing the residents a retreat from the city life.
