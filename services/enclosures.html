Architectural Enclosures

PREMIUM SUSTAINABLE ENCLOSURES IN MULTIPLE MATERIALS AND SYSTEMS, MAKING ARCHITECTURE MORE BEAUTIFUL, COMFORTABLE AND LESS DEPENDENT

TOTAL FACADE SOLUTION: DESIGN, ENGINEERING, FABRICATION, INSTALLATION

Offering a complete solution approach, we take care of everything, from design, fabrication and installation of complex facades with the integration of multiple systems and materials. Thanks to integrating design, engineering and fabrication under one roof, we are able to create complete façade solutions that strike balance between aesthetics, performance and build quality. Each solution responds to the individual requirements of the project, and unlike other façade suppliers, we have the flexibility to work on small private projects as well as larger operations, with multiple materials and systems. As a one single source solution provider, every project is followed closely by our project managers from early stages until completion, in order to meet the highest standards of quality and service.

GLASS FACADES

Glass facades and curtain walls are widely used facade technologies for cladding large areas of a building. We have a flexible design approach that selects the best solution to meet the specific project requirements. If needed, we also develop bespoke solutions.

- Aluminum transom-mullion systems
- Structural glazing
- Steel-glass facades
- Ventilated curtain wall

PERFORMANCE WINDOWS and DOORS

We have the flexibility to manufacture windows and doors in multiple configurations, using different materials and systems. All our windows and doors are custom-built the highest standards of insulation and comfort, based on client and project requirements.

- Windows, doors, sliding leafs for a high degree of comfort and security
- Multiple glazing options
- Large formats and motorized systems
- Multiple frame materials: aluminum, stainless steel, wood.
- Exterior doors in sliding systems, all-glass, concealed, fire resistant.

VENTILATED FACADES

We work with an extensive range of cladding products to suit any budget, aesthetics or special requirements of the project. In association with our extended network of suppliers, we can provide our clients with almost any cladding material that the project might require, such as ceramics, natural timber, stones and composite panel as well as customised panels.

- Ventilated facades with multiple materials
- Bonded facades and decorative cladding
- Free form facades with complex geometries

DOUBLE SKINS & SUN PROTECTION

We develop customized solutions for fixed and dynamic double skins and sun protection systems in multiple materials.

- Sun protection systems with motorized options and active response to the environment
- Lamellae constructions in various materials, static and dynamic options
- Roller shutter in special formats and different degrees of security
- Facade-integrated solutions for glass facades.

INTERNAL GLASS & RAILINGS

The inside of the building is as important as the outside. We offer multiple systems and solutions to solve the internal glazing and metalworks areas of the project.

- Interior and exterior railings combining multiple materials
- Interior glass partitions
- Walkable glass and glass staircases
- Glass folding, sliding walls with concealed profile frame

MODULAR FACADES

Modular facades are an optimal solution for high-speed, high-quality facades, while being tailored to the individual requirements of the project. This facade combines good engineering with a high degree of prefabrication in a controlled facility to minimize the installation works on the construction site. Mondreem modular façades can also be combined seamlessly with other façade systems.

- Curved or tilted facades
- Combination of different materials and glass types
- Large module dimensions
- Integration with sun protection and sound insulation systems
