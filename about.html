Designers, developers, home builders



Mondreem is an international design and development firm, specialized in creating luxury homes, high-end boutiques and bespoke furniture.

Since its inception, Mondreem constantly redefined the impact that design has on the places we live in by building spaces that evoke emotions and enhance the quality of living.

Our expertise extends across the areas of development management, architecture, interior design and specialty construction. In addition, as a result of years of experience in bespoke furniture design and manufacturing, we have created a collection of fine crafted furniture and exclusive products that complement our projects.

WE APPLY OUR STRENGTH IN DESIGN TO DELIVER UNIQUE PROJECTS TO WORLD-CLASS CLIENTS.

Multidisciplinary

As a multidisciplinary firm we integrate our team of designers, architects, engineers, project managers and construction specialists so we can provide solutions to complicated issues and demanding projects.

Complete solution

The range of services and products that we offer goes beyond the ones offered by traditional architecture and design firms. The integration of our design studio with the design-build division allows us to take care of the entire process from early stages of design to completion.

Technology & Materials

Delivering exceptional projects require working closely with leading materials and building systems manufacturers. At Mondreem we cooperate with leading building suppliers in order to provide our clients with the latest solutions available in the market.
